HOW TURUNG SMARTSTORES WORKS

To build and manage your community of customers as subscribers;

Step 1 - Sign-up for a FREE store (account) and set up the wallet

Step 2- Invite friends and family from your phone contacts and social media platforms to subscribe to your store.

Step 3- Select a selling option and start listing/uploading products for rent, book, bid, download/stream etc. Increase product visibility using hashtags (#) or mentioning tags (@).

Buyers (whether within or outside your location) can search for products based on category, brand name, special offers, product colour, proximity, product name, location name, product size and price for patronage without subscribing to your store. Fulfilled customers have a high chance to subscribe to your store for updates.

Step 4- Your subscribers will receive a notification each time you post a new product. They can instantly buy and post a product review, like or share your product post.

Step 5- For every purchase made allows the buyer to write a product review for the item being bought. Alternatively, the buyer can as well navigate to visit your store offline to make patronage. Sellers can reward their subscribers as affiliates for each patronage made through the subscriber's page whenever they share the seller's product.
