The Future of commerce is NOW!

Imagine the impossible

  • Online is already becoming the most preferred place to feature goods and services and experts had estimated that by the year 2040, 95% of purchases will be through e-commerce platforms. Currently, traditional brick and mortar stores have more off-line presence in rural and urban areas compared with their online presence. Turung is designed to provide free online stores to enable off-line stores to sell their products or services beyond their physical location and local currency.

Turung!

With Turung Smartstore your store doesn't just sell, it becomes a mini-bank!

  • Turung is an online smartstore that allows traditional brick and mortar businesses to process crypto-fiat liquidity through a decentralized payment system called TURUpay. With this payment system, imagine cashing out your Shiba Inu straight into fiat (either as cash or through your bank account) at a local store with one-time transaction fees. This will revolutionize how brick and mortar stores operate by giving access to the $3trillion Crypto market.

TURUpay!

With Turung Smartstore your store doesn't just sell, it becomes a mini-bank!

  • TURUpay is a decentralized payment system that supports both cryptocurrency and fiat. Compared with the P2P exchange, TURUpay makes Crypto-fiat payments possible, easy, convenient and fast as it allows users to pay for products or services using cryptocurrencies in a single transaction. TURUpay facilitates all transactions within the Turung ecosystem and, it also serves as a gateway that connects payments between centralized and decentralized platforms.

HOW TURUNG SMARTSTORES WORKS

To build and manage your community of customers as subscribers

  • Step 1 Sign-up for a FREE store (account) and set up a wallet.
  • Step 2 Invite friends and family from your phone contacts and social media platforms to subscribe to your store.
  • Step 3 Provide liquidity by receiving cryptocurrency in exchange for fiat (either physical cash or via betting platforms, lending platforms, Neo/bank accounts etc.) or process crypto payments for utilities (such as DStv, Netflix etc.) and be rewarded in Turu transaction power tokens (TXPR), Turu transaction power tokens can be exchanged for crypto, fiat or Nft's.
  • Step 4 Select a selling option and start listing/uploading products for rent, book, bid, download/stream etc. Increase product visibility using the hashtags (#) or mentioning tags (@).
  • Step 5 Your subscribers will receive a notification each time you post a new product. They can instantly buy and post a product review, like or share your product post. You can reward your subscribers as affiliates.
  • Step 6 The seller fulfills order by product delivery to the buyer. Alternatively, the buyer can navigate to your store offline for patronage.
